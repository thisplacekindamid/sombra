Sombra

Sombra weapons and abilities

  1. Sombra has a submachine gun which she uses as her primary weapon. Her gun is most affective at a close distance.
  2. Sombra's stealth allows her to be invisble to enemies, but if you approach an enemy within a 4 meter raduis you will be detected. Stealth also gives 60% movement speed when invisble.
  3. Sombra's translocator allows her to teleport back to safety. You can cancel a translocator by pressing f.
  4. Sombra's hack allows her to cancel enemies abilities for a temporary time. Her hack lasts 8 seconds and during that time her gun increases damage against the hacked target
  5. EMP is an incredible ultimate, it hacks all targets and removes 40% of their health.

Sombra counters and matchups

Easy matchups for Sombra

  1. Tracer: Tracer relies on her abilities which makes her vulnerable to sombra after a hack or emp blast.
  2. Wrecking ball: same as Tracer Wrecking ball is extremly reliant on his mobility which comes from his abilities.
  3. Bastion: When he is not in his turret form, Bastion can easily be solo killed by using a hack and spraying at him from a close distance.

Sombra's counters

  1. Symmetra: her eyes can obliterate your chances of finding a kill against an enemey.
  2. Reaper: Sombra relies on her submachine gun which can only be used close ranged, exactly where reaper specilizes in.
  3. Zarya: once her weapon is charged she can wipe out your hp immdiately.
